Study and Learn

GCSEs and A Levels are incredibly stressful for students, especially as there is so much content to remember. One of the best ways to prepare (and this does take time!) is retrieval practise - the process of quizzing yourself over and over. To help students do this, we have created the Question Generator - a tool that allows you to create your own quizzes from a bank of hundreds questions written by expert teachers. You can create quizzes from just one topic or multiple topics and download them for yourself or to use with others.

High Quality

Our lessons are pupil-tested. Learn from resources that have helped others achieve 9s at GCSE and A* at A Level!

Up to Date

Easy To Use

Our lessons use software that runs and adapts to whatever device you are using - no programs to download and easy to access anywhere.

Always Available

Want to study on holiday (and who wouldn't?). Now you can! The resources are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

How to use the Question Generator

1

2

Navigate to Question Generator and choose your subject of interest

3

Choose one of the three topic options. Your questions will appear.
